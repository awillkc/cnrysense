The sixth sense, stacked.

Six directions for the CNRYsense / SIXTHsense lockup. The stacked idea is strong — the shared word “sense” becomes the anchor, and the two words above it (CNRY, SIXTH) carry the colour story. Below you'll find specimen pairs, a recommendation, and a starter palette for the full kit.

01 · Stacked lockups

THE PRIMARY IDEA

Two-line type composition. Both words identical in size and weight — only colour differentiates them. “sense” reads as the common spine; “cnry” is the brand, “sixth” is the promise.

03 · Colour system

CANARY + INK + THREE

Yellow and black are the core. I'd recommend adding three supporting tones: a warm paper for marketing surfaces, a soft dusk purple to carry the “sixth sense” strapline, and the three product-state accents from the app (green/amber/red) used only inside the product, never in brand marks.

Why dusk? “Sixth sense” is a mystical-leaning phrase — a second yellow would compete with canary; a red or green would collide with the product states. A muted purple is different enough in hue to separate the strapline without fighting the brand. If you want to stay strictly two-colour, variant A above uses tone-on-tone ink and still works.

04 · Product language

YELLOW RUBBER SENSOR

Soft-touch canary silicone with the bird and wordmark embossed, not printed. No paint to chip, no sticker to peel. Four stainless bolts locate it to the engine block; the rubber absorbs its own vibration so it only hears the engine's.

06 · Usage rules

PROTECTING THE MARK
✓ Do
cnrysense

Keep the two words flush — never space them. “cnry” and “sense” read as one word.

✗ Don't
cnry sense

Don't introduce a space or a dash. CNRYsense is always one word.

✓ Do
cnry

When just the product name is needed, use canary on all four letters.

✗ Don't
cnry

Don't two-tone CNRY on its own. The colour break is reserved for the full lockup.

✓ Do
cnrysense

On dark surfaces, canary stays, black inverts to paper.

✗ Don't
cnrysense

Don't place the primary lockup on dusk — it's reserved for the SIXTHsense line only.
