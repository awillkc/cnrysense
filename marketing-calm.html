How it works

Three signals. One calm verdict.

A sensor mounted to your engine block samples three things continuously. Our models learn what your engine sounds like when it's happy, then watch for drift.

CHANNEL 01

Rhythm

Tri-axis vibration sampled at 4 kHz. Picks up imbalance in the shaft, worn mounts, early bearing wear — weeks before you'd feel them in the tiller.

0.18 mm/s Within baseline
CHANNEL 02

Sound

The acoustic signature of combustion, the alternator, the raw-water pump — each has a tone. When one starts to drift, we hear it before you do.

62 dB · combustion nominal Sounds right
CHANNEL 03

Stress

How hard is the engine working vs. how hard it should be? We combine temperature, RPM and load to tell you when you're outside its sweet spot.

74 °C · 2,340 RPM Comfortable load
